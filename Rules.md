- One big draw pile (UNO style)
  
- 3 Factions:
	1. Demon (Early game, stealing cards)
	2. Human (Late game, buildings to buff units)
	3. Gods (Mid game, hand trap style to block actions)
	   
- Win Condition:
	1. Achieve Attunement -> Discard 5 cards of your factions to get 5 points
	2. Kill enemy Hero

- every turn:
	1. draw 1 card
	2. discard as many as you want -> redraw the same amount
	3. using cards costs Mana
		-> Mana is a card (30% of the entire draw pile, 1 Mana per card)

- Attacking a hero
	- deals damage to hero
	- DEFAULT: attacker loses 1 power
